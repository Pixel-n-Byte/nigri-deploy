Overview

Give your child what they need to grow to be a chossid. Enroll him and her in Cheder Chabad.


Your child will learn the rich history of Chabad Chassidus, about the Rebbes of Lubavitch and their love for their chassidim. They will learn the meaning of ahavas yisroel and how to learn torah and do mitzvos b’hidur. They’ll be inspired to connect with the Rebbe and to do all they can to bring us to the time of the ge’ula shleima!

Schedule

Cheder Chabad take place for an hour a day, between Tefillah Club and Day School.

What happens at Cheder?

  • Our students learn themes in chassidus

  • Learn about chassidishe milestones and yomim tovim

  • Participate in farbrengens

  • Memorize Tanya b’al peh

  • Lots more!

At Cheder Chabad your child will learn the deep passion of what makes a Chossid!
