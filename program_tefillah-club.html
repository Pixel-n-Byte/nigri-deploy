Overview

Start your child’s day in the best way. Regardless of where and when your child goes to school, if they don’t have tefillah in their school/class, have them join Tefillah Club.


Each day at Tefillah Club your child will learn the meaning and inspiration of davening, and will then daven with a group of friends.

Details

  • Tefillah groups are separated by age and by nusach (tradition).

  • Tefillah Club is open to kids who learn at NJOS or anywhere else!

Watch your kid’s excitement when it’s their turn to be Chazzan or Chazzanit!
