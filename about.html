The JOS Mission

Simply put, the Torah belongs to every Jew.

We’re here to make that happen.

Whether you’re looking for a full time Day School, a weekly enrichment class, or something in between, we are here for you.

Your child’s Torah learning can flourish from the comfort of your home. We work to meet your child where they’re at, giving them access to quality Jewish education and lifelong connections with children they share common values with. We are here for every Jewish child - any age, in any place, and at any time.

At the Nigri Jewish Online School your child will:

  • Receive a top-notch Jewish education and love it too!

  • Enjoy extra-curricular programs out of the classroom.

  • Feel the warmth and care of our teachers through the screen and beyond.

  • Meet Jewish friends from around the world.

  • Receive a birthday package and prizes in the mail.

School Pillars

The world stands on three pillars. Torah, Avodah & Gemilas Chasadim.


Jewish Online School encourages your child to increase in their Torah learning, in their service of G-d & acts of loving kindness to their fellow friends.

Global

Teaching children from all across the globe, from the United States & Canada to to all six continents. Classes are held at various times to accommodate your child.

Growth Mindset

Helping your child grow through encouragement & compliments, so they can reach their potential in a positive way.
